Beechcraft King Air C-90

The King Air C90 gives you access to short and uncontrolled airstrips… Lets you go places others never will!

The King Air C90 is arguably the most popular and versatile turboprop aircraft in the world, beaten only by the Citation XLS, Falcon 2000, and Citation CJ2.

  • 272 knots maximum cruise speed; more than 1,200 nautical mile range
  • A square-oval cabin cross-section lets passengers sit up straight in quintessential King Air comfort

Cessna Citation XL

Featuring an advanced composite carbon-fibre/ epoxy honeycomb fuselage the aircraft allows greater (approx 13%) internal cabin space compared to the competition.

Main cabin can seat 9 passengers in club configuration with divan opposite the cabin door. Toilet is in aft cabin opposite large area for cargo and closet area for supplies. It has the ability to operate out of smaller airports while still offering the comfort and amenities of a midsize jet offering a unique combination of performance, range, comfort and capacity.

  • Features a full refreshment center, modern in-flight entertainment system, satellite communications
  • Largest baggage compartment in its class

Bombardier Global 6000

The Global 6000 aircraft answers the needs of the world’s most discerning travelers for the ultimate luxury experience. Arrive at your destination refreshed and ready—and perhaps even looking forward to the return flight home.

The ultra-long range G6000 aircraft can cruise at an outstanding Mach 0.88 and fly an impressive 6,000 nautical miles with a complement of 8 passengers and a full baggage compartment. Impressive short takeoff and landing distances in its class bring greater access to more challenging airfields. Its steep approach capability makes it the only aircraft in its class certified to fly in and out of the centrally located London City Airport.

Dassault Falcon 2000LX

The ultimate combination of range, agility and comfort

The Falcon 2000LXS can land at over 90% of its maximum takeoff weight. You can make multiple short hops and still have plenty of non-stop, long-distance range, without refueling. Save money. Save time. Do what others can’t.
The superior agility of the new 4,000 nm Falcon 2000LXS gives you access to challenging airfields. In fact, no other jet in its category can deliver so many airports and deliver so much range out of them.

Embraer ERJ 135

The ERJ’s three abreast configuration and large windows result in a spacious cabin offering 2 aisle and 2 window seating.

Overhead stowage feature space-saving retractable panels & large wardrobes allow for additional stowage. Five- galley arrangement option allows for customized in-flight catering and cabin service.

  • Capacity: 2 crew + 2 Airhostess + 37 Passengers
  • Cruise speed: 0.78 mach / 447 kt
  • Range: 1,300 NM / 2,409 km

Pilatus PC-12

Large, powerful, and efficient, the Pilatus PC-12 NG is a versatile aircraft that delivers benefits across the boardOne of the most versatile in the world, the PC-12 NG is bigger than a King Air 250, over twice as large as a Citation Mustang, and has a max payload of over 1300 kgs.

  • A high-lift wing for exceptional short-field performance
  • Standard forward passenger door and large (53 in X 52 in) aft cargo door
  • Single-pilot certification
  • Direct operating cost about half that of most business jet competitorsy

Bell 407

The Bell 407 integrates reliability, speed and performance for a variety of missions. It provides a very quiet and smooth ride in virtually all weather conditions.

Superb hot & high performance and all-composite 4-blade main rotor system with “softinplane” hub for excellent ride quality. Abundant capacity for specialized equipment (medical, search lights, etc.) with nearly 4 hrs of loitering capability.

  • Capacity: 1 Pilot and 6 Passengers
  • Cruise speed: 133 kts / 246 kph
  • Range: 330 nm / 611 km
